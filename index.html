ABOUT INDORE MARATHON

Academy of Indore Marathoners (A unit of Malwa Marathoners, Registered under MP Society Act. Reg. No. : 03/27/03/16586/13) is a nonprofit organization that was formed to conduct Marathon in Indore every year from time to time to all category of people i.e. Full time, part time, Dream Runners, Veterans, or disability to help increase grassroots participation for a safe, enjoyable and learning experience. We aim to achieve this through the widest voluntary collaboration with the existing bodies and local associates of Indore.
